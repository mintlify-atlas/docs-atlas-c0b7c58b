SmartMove is your real-time public transit companion for South Tyrol (Südtirol / Alto Adige). Whether you're commuting daily or exploring the region, SmartMove gives you instant access to connections across trains, buses, cable cars, and the Bolzano tram — with live departure data, GPS route following, and service alerts.

## What SmartMove does

SmartMove connects to the official South Tyrol transit authority API (`efa.sta.bz.it`) to deliver real-time data across the entire regional network. It works fully offline using local storage — cloud sync is optional via a free account.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Navigate to the SmartMove web app. No installation required — it runs in any modern browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search your first connection">
    Type your departure and destination stops on the home screen. SmartMove autocompletes stop names in both German and Italian.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow your route (optional)">
    Tap any result to see the full route detail, then enable route following for real-time walking progress and next-stop notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  SmartMove works fully without an account. Create a free account to sync your favorites, saved routes, and settings across all your devices.
</Note>
