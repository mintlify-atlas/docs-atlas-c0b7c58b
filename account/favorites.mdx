SmartMove lets you save the stops, connections, and routes you use most so you can reach them in one tap. Favorites are stored on your device and sync to the cloud when you are signed in.

## Favorite stops

Any stop you have recently searched can be marked as a favorite.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for a stop">
    Use the route search or the departure board search to look up a stop. SmartMove adds it to your recent stops list automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the star icon">
    In your recent stops list (visible on the home screen and in the Favorites screen), tap the **star** or **heart** icon next to a stop name to mark it as a favorite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find it in Favorites">
    Open **Account → Favorites (Favoriten)**. Your favorited stops appear in the **Stops** section at the top of the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When you favorite a stop, SmartMove automatically adds it to your **Quick Access** items so it is pinned to your home screen as well.

To unfavorite a stop, tap the star icon again. It is removed from your favorites and from quick access.

## Favorite connections

A connection is a from/to pair — for example, Bolzano → Merano. Connections you search are saved to your recent connections list, and you can mark any of them as favorites.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search a connection">
    Enter a departure and destination on the home screen and run a search. The connection is added to your recent connections automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mark it as a favorite">
    In your recent connections list, tap the **heart** icon next to the connection to mark it as a favorite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access it instantly">
    Favorited connections appear in **Account → Favorites** and are added to quick access. Tap the connection there to pre-fill the route search form and jump straight to results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Liked routes

Liking a route bookmarks a specific journey — not just a from/to pair, but the full itinerary including the departure time, transport mode, and duration.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a connection">
    Run a route search and review the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Like a result">
    Tap the **heart** icon on a result card in the list, or open the route detail view and tap the heart icon there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a liked route">
    Go to **Account → Favorites** and tap any liked route to open its full detail view immediately. SmartMove restores the complete itinerary so you can review stops, times, and delays.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each liked route stores:

- The **from** and **to** stop names
- The primary **transport mode** (train, bus, or cable car)
- The **duration** of the journey
- The **full itinerary** so you can open it and start following it directly

<Tip>
  Liked routes are great for journeys you take regularly but at different times of day — bookmark the route once, then open it to check live departure times whenever you need it.
</Tip>

## Quick access

Quick access lets you pin stops, lines, or routes to the top of your home screen for instant retrieval. Items appear as chips or cards directly on the home screen so you never need to search for them again.

Items are added to quick access automatically when you:

- Favorite a stop
- Favorite a connection

You can also manage quick access items directly:

- To **remove** a quick access item, open **Account → Favorites**, find the item in the quick access section, and tap the remove or trash icon. Removing a stop from quick access also removes its favorite flag.
- Reordering is not currently supported — items appear in the order they were added.

Quick access item types:

| Type | What it represents |
|------|--------------------|
| **Stop** | A transit stop, shown with a map pin icon |
| **Line** | A specific bus or train line |
| **Route** | A from → to connection pair |

## Viewing and managing all favorites

Open the **Account** tab and tap **Favorites (Favoriten)** to see everything you have saved in one place.

The Favorites screen is divided into three sections:

1. **Liked routes** — Full itineraries you have bookmarked, shown with their transport mode icon, from/to names, and duration
2. **Favorite stops** — Individual stops marked as favorites, shown with a map pin icon
3. **Favorite connections** — From/to pairs you have saved, shown with an arrow between the stop names

Tap any item to open it. Tap the **trash** icon to remove it permanently.

## Route sharing

You can share any route detail view as a short link. The link encodes the full itinerary and can be opened by anyone — the recipient does not need a SmartMove account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a route detail view">
    Tap any connection result to open the full trip breakdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the share icon">
    Tap the **share** icon in the top right corner of the detail view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy or send the link">
    SmartMove generates a short link in the format `/r/:id`. Share it via any messaging app, email, or copy it to your clipboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The recipient opens the link and sees the full route — including every leg, stop, and time — without needing to sign in.

<Note>
  All favorites work fully offline. Your stops, connections, and liked routes are stored on your device and accessible without an internet connection. They sync to the cloud in the background when you are signed in and online.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many favorites can I save?">
    There is no hard limit on liked routes or favorited connections. Recent stops are capped at the 10 most recent, so only the 10 stops you have searched most recently can be favorited. Searching a stop again moves it to the top of the list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="If I remove a favorite, is it gone from my cloud too?">
    Yes. Removing a favorite updates both your local device and your cloud data (if you are signed in). The change syncs immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I favorite a stop I haven't searched yet?">
    Not directly — a stop must appear in your recent stops list before you can favorite it. Search for it once and it will appear in your recents, where you can then mark it as a favorite.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do share links expire?">
    Share links do not have a set expiry. However, they depend on the itinerary data being available in the SmartMove database. If the underlying data is removed, the link will no longer load correctly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
