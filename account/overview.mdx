SmartMove works completely without a login. Every stop you search, route you save, and preference you set is stored directly on your device and available instantly — no account required.

When you do create an account, everything you have already saved carries over. Your data syncs to the cloud so it is available on every device you use to sign in.

## What you get with an account

| Without an account | With an account |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| Full route search and live departures | All the same features |
| Favorites, liked routes, quick access | Favorites backed up to the cloud |
| Commuter mode shortcuts | Commuter routes synced across devices |
| Theme and language settings | Theme and language synced across devices |
| Route sharing | Route sharing |
| Works fully offline | Works offline, syncs when online |

<Note>
  If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data without an account, your saved data is lost. Signing in backs everything up so you never lose your favorites.
</Note>

## Your account screen

Open the **Account** tab (the person icon in the bottom navigation) to manage your profile and settings. The screen shows a count of your current favorites and recent routes at a glance.

From the account screen you can also switch between **Light**, **Dark**, and **System** themes using the three icons at the top.
