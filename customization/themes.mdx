SmartMove supports three appearance modes. You can switch at any time from the account screen and the change takes effect immediately across the entire app.

## Theme options

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Light** | Always uses the light colour scheme |
| **Dark** | Always uses the dark colour scheme |
| **System** | Follows your operating system's current setting |

<Tip>
  Use **System** if you have your OS configured to switch between light and dark automatically at sunrise and sunset — SmartMove will follow that schedule without any extra steps.
</Tip>

## How to change your theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Account tab">
    Tap the **Account** tab (the person icon in the bottom navigation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a theme">
    At the top of the account screen you will see three icons representing **Light**, **Dark**, and **System**. Tap the one you want.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The theme switches instantly. You do not need to reload the page or restart the app.

## How theme is stored

Your theme choice is saved to your device under the key `smartmove-theme`. It is applied before the app finishes loading, so you will never see a flash of the wrong theme on startup.

When the theme changes, the app fires a `theme-changed` event on the `window` object. If you are building an integration or a browser extension on top of SmartMove, you can listen for this event to respond to theme changes in real time.

If you are signed in to a SmartMove account, your theme preference syncs to the cloud and is restored on every device you sign in to.
